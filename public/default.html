URL Shortener Service

Example usage

Input:

https://backend-projects-gleutheuser.c9users.io/new/www.google.com

Output:

{"originalURL":"www.google.com","shortenedURL":"https://backend-projects-gleutheuser.c9users.io/866"}

Result:

https://backend-projects-gleutheuser.c9users.io/866 will redirect to www.google.com.
